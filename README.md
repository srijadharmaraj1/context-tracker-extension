# 🧠 Context Tracker Extension – Smart Context & Productivity Tracker

**Context Tracker Extension** is a Chrome Extension that helps you stay focused by **tracking your browsing context**, analyzing your behavior, and managing tasks based on your intent. It’s more than a productivity tool—it’s a **context-aware assistant** for your online activities.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 🎯 Intent Tracking
- Add tasks (intent) while browsing
- Support multiple tasks at once
- Mark tasks as completed with a checkbox
- Completed tasks auto-remove after 24 hours  

---

### 📊 Context & Activity Tracking
- Tracks **time spent on each website**
- Records **tab behavior and browsing context**
- Displays recent activity with **visual progress bars**
- Converts time into readable format (sec/min/hr)  

---

### 🧠 Insights Engine
- Detects your **focus mode**:
  - 🔥 Deep focus  
  - 📖 Reading  
  - 👀 Quick browsing  
- Provides **instant feedback** on your behavior  

---

### ⚠️ Smart Reminders
- Detects distraction (YouTube, Instagram, etc.)
- Alerts if you’re stuck on a page (>5 mins)
- Shows **focus status**: Focused / Distracted  

---

### 🧠 Task + Context Integration
- Tasks appear alongside behavioral insights
- Connects **what you planned** vs **what you actually did**  

---

### 🔥 Streak System
- Tracks number of active days
- Motivates consistency and habit formation  

---

### 🔔 Live Alerts
- Real-time popup alerts for:
  - Distraction  
  - Over-staying on a page  
  - Task completion  

---

## ⚙️ How It Works
1. Open any website  
2. Enter your **intent** (task)  
3. Browse normally  
4. Extension tracks:
   - Time spent
   - Tab behavior and context  
5. View dashboard:
   - Activity & insights
   - Reminders
   - Tasks  

---

## 🧹 Auto Cleanup
- Completed tasks are automatically removed after 24 hours
- Keeps your dashboard clean and relevant
